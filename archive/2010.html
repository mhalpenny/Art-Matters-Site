“DEAR ART MATTERS:
WE WILL TURN CONTROL LAB INTO A BASEMENT BOY PLACE WITH INSTANT NOODLE SOUP, TV W/ DREAMCAST OR SOME SHITTY SYSTEM, A BONG, LOTS OF SHITTY COUCHES + CUSHIONS MAYBE SOME AIR HOCKEY OR PINGPONG AND A CRAPPY STEREO SYSTEM AND LOTS OF SHITTY FOOD - MAYBE SOME DRAWINGS / ART / POSTERS / GRAF ABOUT WEED + GIRLS + ALT MUSIC + UTOPIC SCI FI + HEROES.
