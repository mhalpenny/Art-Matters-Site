CONFESSIONS:
JOHNATHAN REID SÉVIGNY

Feb. 17 fév. 2014
Concordia University



Concordia Grad (BFA 2013) Johnathan Reid Sévingy recently release his first artist monograph “The Sweetsburg Archives” this winter. He gave a talk at Concordia University to reveal the highs and lows of living as a twenty-something post-grad artist guy in 2014. In Confessions, not only did he unlock the backstories of the various personas within his multi-media works, he also confessed the sometimes-busted reality of maintaining art world relevance while teaching high school art classes in rural Québec.
