CONSENT. A DISCUSSION.

Date: September 12, 2018
Location: VAV Gallery
Time: 15h - 17h

In collaboration with the Sexual Assault Resource Centre and the Dean of Students, FASA and Art Matters will be partnering up to facilitate a workshop on issues of power and consent. Held in the VAV Gallery, directly after the FASA Barbecue, this workshop will encourage open conversation around these issues, specifically within the University context and Fine Arts milieu. This workshop is open to Concordia students from all disciplines!
